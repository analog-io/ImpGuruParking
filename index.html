Imp.Guru is becoming analog.io!

Over the past year it has been my distinct pleasure to provide a free and easy to use plotting tool for your data.sparkfun.com data streams. Recently I have been notified that ninefold.com, our hosting site, has discontinued the Ruby on Rails hosting services that enabled Imp.Guru to be available. Due to this transition, I have made the hard decision to also discontinue imp.guru as a service and instead focus on a new solution that has more functionality and features. This service is called analog.io and it is available today. You may Sign up for analog.io here.

I'm very greatful for the success that imp.guru has been and most importanly the users who have made it come to life! I hope to continue this tradition with the analog.io community!
